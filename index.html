What We Do

Garden care, done properly.

We cut your grass, trim your hedges, and keep your borders weed-free - simple as that. No jargon, no fuss, just a garden you'll actually want to spend time in.

Grass Cutting

A neat lawn makes the whole garden sing. We mow, edge, and blow away the clippings - leaving you with those satisfying stripes every time.

Hedge Cutting

Sharp, clean lines that make your property look cared for from the kerb. We handle all heights and shapes, including the tricky ones against walls and fences.

Border Work & Weeding

Overgrown borders drain a garden's character. We get in among the plants, clear the weeds, and give your beds room to breathe and look their best.

Garden Clearances

Inherited a jungle? Moving home? We clear overgrown gardens completely - all waste is removed using our licensed waste carrier licence, responsibly disposed of.

Garden Tidy Ups

Sometimes a garden just needs a proper sort-out - deadheading, pruning, sweeping, and bagging. A few hours with us and it looks like a different place.

Seasonal Preparation

Get your garden ready for whatever's coming - spring feeding and mulching, autumn leaf clearance, or winter cutting-back. We keep things healthy year-round.
